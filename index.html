Group standings

Sorted by points, then goal difference, then goals scored. This is a simplified sort — FIFA's official tiebreakers also use head-to-head results and fair-play conduct points before falling back to the world ranking, so exact order among tied teams may differ slightly until more matches are played.

Third-place race

Eight of the twelve third-place finishers join the 24 group winners and runners-up in the Round of 32. Ranked the same way as above, across all 12 groups. Which exact eight qualify also decides who each group winner draws below — FIFA pairs them using a fixed table covering all 495 possible combinations of qualifiers, applied live here.

Knockout bracket — assuming the groups end today

The Round of 32 below is filled in completely: the 8 winner-vs-runner-up pairings come straight from the live standings above, and the other 8 third-place matchups use FIFA's actual published assignment table for whichever specific combination of eight third-place teams currently qualifies. From the Round of 16 on, nobody knows the results yet — click a team in any match to advance them, and everything downstream updates automatically.
